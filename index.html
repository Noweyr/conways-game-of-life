Welcome to Conway's Game of Life!

    How to play:

  1. Select which cells are initially alive or dead by clicking on them - or click "Randomize"! Green is alive, gray is dead.

  2. Press the "Run" button!

  3. Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbours dies by underpopulation.

  4. Any live cell with two or three live neighbours lives on to the next generation.

  5. Any live cell with more than three live neighbours dies by overpopulation.

  6. Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbours becomes a live cell through reproduction.
